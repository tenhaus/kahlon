about KAHLON:

challenges the boundaries of genre specific music events and present new wave artists with distinctive styles on one stage to a colorful influx of music enthusiast to cultivate a fresh take on traditional showcasing.

KAHLON is curated by Baltimore-based rapper Abdu Ali with assistance by True Laurels editor Lawrence Burney.
